Process

I think this needs a proper section because process talks about how we work and how we face problems. It's also a mean to connect each other when working as a team.

So, this is roughly my process when I have to work in a solution to a given problem:

Input phase

Find out your business needs. Find out your users needs. Collect facts and data. Understand previous mistakes.

Hands-on phase

Sketch and wireframe possible ideas. Usability test them (professionally or guerrilla). Design and code (yay!).

Polishing phase

A/B test. Be prepared to start over if your new design's conversion rates are lower than the previous ones. Iterate. Then iterate again.
